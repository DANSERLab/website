RF Network Localization Method for Unmanned Robotics Systems

A study towards developing a Global Positioning System (GPS) by using Recieved Signal Strength Indication (RSSI) between several stationary nodes and a mobile node to predict the position of the mobile noded. The goals of the paper are to find the optimal localization algorithm and intentify an optimal number of stationary nodes. The proposed technique is affordable and can be successfully used in electronically noisy environments.
