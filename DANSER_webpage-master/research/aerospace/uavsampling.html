Distributed Sampling Using Smalle Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) for Scientific Missions

The goal of this project is to develop a flexible, decentralized network of small and inexpensive, battery-powered unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV), which could be used for a variety of scientific missions where measurements from a distributed network of nodes could significantly improve the assessment (e.g. chemical or dust clouds, mapping particulates, chemical content and physical boundaries of a dispersed volcanic plume or vog).
