Bioreactor

Designing an anatomical bioreactor to prolong viability of cortical organoid models for human brain study. Specifically, mass-production of tissue samples for research in brain development, treatment testing, gene-knockout experiments with CRISPR-based gene editing, facilitation of live neural network study, and brain-computer interfacing.
