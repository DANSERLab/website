Virtual and Augmented Reality

We look at ways we can use the booming field of virtual and augmented reality to furhter various physical therapy treatments. With state of the art technology and dedicated researchers this is one of the fastest expanding topics of research here at UCSC. This group works in conjunction with ASSIST Labs here at UCSC as well as Dominican Health to test our prospective treatments on real life patients
